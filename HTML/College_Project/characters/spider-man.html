Spider‑Man

Peter Parker, a teenager bitten by a radioactive spider, gains superhuman abilities and becomes the friendly neighborhood Spider‑Man. Balancing hero duties with everyday life, he’s a key young Avenger.

Abilities

  • Wall-crawling & super strength
  • Spider-sense (danger detection)
  • Web-shooters and gadgets
  • Quick reflexes and agility

Key Movies

  1. Spider‑Man: Homecoming (2017)
  2. Avengers: Infinity War (2018)
  3. Spider‑Man: No Way Home (2021)
