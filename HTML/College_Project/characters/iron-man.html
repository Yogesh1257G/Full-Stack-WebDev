Iron Man

Tony Stark, a billionaire genius, becomes Iron Man after building a high-tech suit of armor. Known for his wit, intelligence, and leadership, he’s one of the founding Avengers and a key figure in defending Earth.

Abilities

  • Powered armor suit with flight & weapons
  • Super-genius intellect
  • Advanced AI & tech expertise
  • Arc reactor-powered heart
