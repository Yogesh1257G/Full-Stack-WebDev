Thor

Thor, the Norse God of Thunder, wields the magical hammer Mjölnir. As the prince of Asgard, he protects both his realm and Earth, often alongside the Avengers.

Abilities

  • God-like strength and durability
  • Control over lightning and storms
  • Wields Mjölnir & Stormbreaker
  • Immortal Asgardian physiology

Key Movies

  1. Thor: Ragnarok (2017)
  2. Avengers: Infinity War (2018)
  3. Thor: Love and Thunder (2022)
