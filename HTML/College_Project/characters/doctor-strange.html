Doctor Strange

Dr. Stephen Strange, once a brilliant but arrogant neurosurgeon, becomes the Sorcerer Supreme after a life-changing accident. He now guards reality from mystical threats.

Abilities

  • Mastery of the mystic arts
  • Time manipulation (Eye of Agamotto)
  • Teleportation and astral projection
  • Shielding Earth from magical threats

Key Movies

  1. Doctor Strange (2016)
  2. Avengers: Infinity War (2018)
  3. Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness (2022)
